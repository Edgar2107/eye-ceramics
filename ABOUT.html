ABOUT

Edgar Escobar is my name. I am currently working out of my home studio in the state of Utah. Ceramics became part of my life during my sophmore year of high school in 2008. Ever since then I've been throwing and firing in between seasons. I specialize in custom glazed low fire ceramics and raku fired ceramics. View some of my previous work by clicking on one of the buttons above. Recent for sale items will be listed in the corisponding button above. There will be a green border around the image if available for purchase. Pieces are avaible as individuals or as pair unless specified otherwise.


Raku ware is an alternative method of a low fire technique that originated in Japan. My custom ceramic pieces have been glazed and fired to a cone 04 bisque temperature. I am currently working w/ naked raku, horse hair raku, sodium silicate resin texture and glazed raku firings. These ceramic pots are capable of holding liquid, but NOT recommended for consumable liquids and food. These pieces are fired in a propane or natural gas fueled kiln and has been exposed to lead based glazes. Lead can be toxic for human consumption and cause serious health issues, or possibly death if used for serving food and water. This method of firing is used for HOME DECOR ONLY.
