ABOUT & CONTACT

Edgar Escobar is my name. I am currently working out of my home studio in the state of Utah. Ceramics became part of my life during my sophmore year of high school in 2008. Ever since then, I've been throwing and firing in between seasons. I specialize in custom, glazed, low fire, ceramics and raku fired ceramics.
